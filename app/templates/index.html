Technology — how it works

A modular toolkit combining sensing, logic, and effectors inside an optimized delivery system.

Sensing
Detect transcripts/proteins that define disease states.
Logic
AND/OR/NOT circuits minimize off‑target activity.
Effectors
Control expression, immune tuning, or apoptosis.
Delivery
AAV serotypes mapped to tissue biodistribution.
